Corona Checkpoints.
Keep the following things in mind.

A mask is a must.


Always remember to wear a mask. Also ensure your mouth and nose are properly covered by the mask.

Wash, wash and wash again..


Keep washing your hands at regular intervals. Spend at least 20 seconds rubbing to ensure that you cover all the nooks and corners.

Remember the 6 Feet Rule.


Always ensure a minimum gap of 2 meters (or 6 feet) between you and others. This ensures that the droplet spread of this disease is minimised.

Sneeze... But ensure your mouth is covered


Follow the right etiquettes while coughing and sneezing. Always use a tissue or a cloth to cover your mouth while sneezing. If a tissue is not available, use your elbow to cover your mouth.

Always keep a sanitiser handy


Remember to thoroughly sanitise your hands after you touch any surface. The picture on the left shows how to use a sanitiser correctly.

Watch out for symptoms


Check if you or your family members have any of the following symptoms. If you suspect chances of having come in contact with a Coronavirus patient, seek medical help immediately.
